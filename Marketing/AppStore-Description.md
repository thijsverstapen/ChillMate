## Description (limit 4000)

ChillMate is a calm, private companion for looking after yourself, with no judgment and nothing to prove. Whether you are planning ahead, checking in with yourself, or finding your feet again afterwards, it keeps the things that matter in one private overview that never leaves your iPhone.

No ads. No tracking. Nothing sold. Just a quiet space that is yours.

PLAN WITH A CLEAR HEAD
- Build a short plan before a session: ending time, transport home, medication, limits, protection, and who to call.
- Keep harm-reduction information from verified, official public-health sources close by.
- Check timing and combinations so you can make more informed choices.

LOOK AFTER YOUR SEXUAL HEALTH
- Track STI test dates and results, and set reminders for follow-ups.
- Keep PrEP and PEP timing organised, including the windows that actually matter.
- Prepare partner-notification messages on your terms, only sent when you choose.

KNOW WHERE YOU STAND
- A private overview of your last three months: check-ins, sleep, substances, and aftercare in one calm view.
- Gentle, non-judgmental insights that show patterns as neutral signals, never as a score to feel bad about.
- Optional Apple Health sync for sleep, heart rate, HRV, and workouts, only for the categories you allow.

RECOVER AND REFLECT
- Aftercare prompts and a recovery mode for the days that follow.
- A craving-delay tool and private journal for the harder moments.
- Daily affirmations if you want a softer landing when you open the app.

HELP WHEN YOU NEED IT
- Panic support with breathing and grounding.
- An emergency card, a trusted contact, and a one-tap location message.
- Safe Route to help you get home, and quick links to crisis, sexual-health, and addiction-care services.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
- Your profile, logs, plans, and notes are stored on your iPhone.
- Add a Face ID or PIN lock, and turn on discreet notifications so lock-screen wording stays vague.
- Optional encrypted backup to your own iCloud, accessible only to you.
- No advertising, no analytics trackers, and your health and sexual-health details are never sold or shared.

ChillMate is a wellbeing and reflection tool. It does not encourage substance use or sex, it does not diagnose, and it does not decide whether anything is safe. Its information is drawn from trusted public-health sources and is meant to support, not replace, a conversation with a qualified professional. If someone may be in immediate danger, call your local emergency number.

Free, made with care, and built for adults (18+).
