# Privacy Policy — ChillMate

**Last updated: 29 May 2026**
**Developer: Thijs Verstappen**
**Contact: tjw.verstappen@gmail.com**

---

## Overview

ChillMate is a private, on-device harm-reduction companion. The app is designed from the ground up so that **all data stays on your device**. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information — ever.

This policy explains what data the app stores locally, what it does not do, and your rights as a user.

---

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect

ChillMate does **not**:

- Create or require a user account
- Send any data to external servers or the developer
- Use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
- Access Apple Health or HealthKit
- Track your location in the background
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies or web tracking of any kind

---

## 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Everything you enter into ChillMate is stored privately in your device's local database (Apple SwiftData / SQLite). This includes:

| Data type | What it contains | Stored |
|---|---|---|
| Night logs | Dates, substances noted, mood, sleep hours, risk flags | On-device only |
| User profile | First name, preferences, onboarding choices | On-device only |
| Safer-session plans | Checklist entries, planned substances, contacts | On-device only |
| Drug timer records | Start/end times, substance labels, check-in notes | On-device only |
| STI / sexual health test records | Test dates, result status by site | On-device only |
| Journal entries | Free-text notes, dates | On-device only |
| Recovery scores & streaks | Calculated wellness scores | On-device only |
| Emergency information | Trusted contacts, ICE details, location notes | On-device only |
| App preferences | Background style, accessibility settings | On-device only |

None of this data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly use the **backup/export** feature, in which case the encrypted file goes only where you choose to send it (e.g. your iCloud Drive or AirDrop recipient).

---

## 3. Sensitive Health Information

ChillMate may store information that is sensitive in nature, including substance use records and sexual health test results. This data:

- **Is never transmitted** to any server, cloud service, or third party
- **Is stored only in the app's private SwiftData container** on your device
- **Is not accessible** to other apps unless you explicitly export a backup
- **Is deleted** when you delete the app from your device

If you use iCloud Backup, your device's standard iCloud Backup may include the app's local database as part of the full device backup. This is controlled by your iCloud settings, not by ChillMate.

---

## 4. Location Data

ChillMate requests location access solely for the **Emergency Information** feature, which lets you include your current location in an emergency message to a trusted contact. Location is:

- Accessed only when you actively open the Emergency screen and choose to include it
- Never collected in the background
- Never stored persistently or sent to any server

---

## 5. Notifications

If you enable check-in or recovery reminders, ChillMate schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external push notification service.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

ChillMate includes no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or social login integrations. The app makes no outbound network requests under normal operation.

---

## 7. Age Restriction

ChillMate is intended for adults aged 18 and over. It is not directed at children or minors. If you are under 18, please do not use this app.

---

## 8. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored on your device:

- **To delete all data**: delete the ChillMate app from your iPhone. All local data is permanently removed.
- **To delete specific entries**: use the in-app delete options within the Calendar or Journal screens.

There is no account to close and no server-side data to request deletion of.
