## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting started

**What is ChillMate?**
ChillMate is a private harm-reduction companion for adults who use substances. It helps you stay safer before, during, and after a night out — with care tools, safety features, personal logs, and private insights. No account, no cloud, no judgment.

**Does ChillMate require an account?**
No. The app works entirely on your device with no registration, login, or internet connection required.

**Is my data private?**
Yes — completely. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted anywhere. See the full [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

**What iOS version do I need?**
ChillMate requires iOS 26 or later and an iPhone.

### Logging

**How do I log a night?**
Tap **+ Add** on the Home screen or go to the **Home** tab and tap the log button. You can record substances, sleep, mood, and any notes about how you felt.

**Can I edit or delete a log entry?**
Yes. Open the **Calendar** tab, find the entry by date, and tap it to edit or delete it.

**What is a "skip"?**
A skip is a logged night where you chose not to use substances. Tracking skips helps you see your patterns over time and contributes positively to your wellness score.

---

### Care Tools

**What is Safety Autopilot?**
Safety Autopilot looks at your recent logs and current situation and suggests the single most relevant action — for example, starting a recovery check-in, reviewing your safer-use plan, or contacting a trusted person.

**How does Panic Support work?**
Panic Support guides you through breathing exercises and grounding techniques when you're feeling overwhelmed. It also gives you one-tap access to a trusted contact.

**What is the safer-use plan (Plan)?**
The Plan tool is a before-session checklist. You can note what you intend to use, set intentions, add a trusted contact's number, and review harm-reduction reminders before a night out.

**How do drug check-in timers work?**
Check-in timers let you set reminders during a session — for example, to drink water, check in with a friend, or assess how you're feeling. You receive local notifications at the intervals you choose.

**What does Recovery Mode do?**
Recovery Mode helps you track goals and manage cravings during a break or recovery period. You can log how you're feeling each day and monitor your streak.

---

### Insights

**What is the Risk Checker?**
The Risk Checker flags potential combination risks based on the substances you've logged. It is for informational reference only and does not replace professional medical advice.

**What is the wellness score?**
Your daily wellness score is a private number calculated from your recent logs — taking into account sleep, frequency, recovery days, and other factors. It is for personal reflection only and has no clinical meaning.

**What is the Helper Bridge?**
Helper Bridge generates a plain-language summary of your recent patterns that you can share with a trusted friend, partner, or healthcare provider. You choose what to include and who to send it to.

### Emergency Information

**What does the Emergency Information feature store?**
You can save trusted contact details, an ICE (In Case of Emergency) message, and optional location notes. This information is stored only on your device.

**How do I send an emergency message?**
Open **Care Tools → Emergency Information**, review your details, and tap **Send message**. This opens your Messages app with a pre-filled message — you confirm before sending.

### Privacy & Data

**How do I delete all my data?**
Delete ChillMate from your iPhone. All data is permanently removed from your device. There is no server to contact.

**Can I back up my data?**
Yes. Go to **More → Settings → Backup** to export an encrypted backup file. You can save it to iCloud Drive, AirDrop it, or store it anywhere you choose.

**Will my data be included in an iCloud Backup?**
If you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iPhone settings, your device's full backup may include ChillMate's local database as part of the system backup. This is controlled by your iCloud settings, not by ChillMate.

### Troubleshooting

**The app is running slowly.**
Try closing and reopening the app. If it persists, restart your iPhone. If you have a very large number of log entries, performance may improve after archiving older entries in Settings.

**My notifications aren't arriving.**
Go to **iPhone Settings → Notifications → ChillMate** and confirm that notifications are allowed. Inside ChillMate, check **More → Settings** to make sure reminders are enabled.

**The wellness score seems wrong.**
The score is recalculated each time you open the app based on your recent logs. If you've recently added or deleted entries, the score will update on the next app launch.

**I lost my data after restoring my iPhone.**
Data is restored from your iCloud Backup if one was made. If you used ChillMate's built-in encrypted backup, restore it via **More → Settings → Restore Backup** and select your backup file.

**The app won't open / crashes on launch.**
Try deleting and reinstalling the app. If you have an encrypted backup, you can restore your data afterwards. If the problem persists, email us with your iPhone model and iOS version.

### Medical & Safety

**Is ChillMate medical advice?**
No. ChillMate is a private reflection tool. It does not diagnose conditions, recommend substance use, provide dosage guidance, or replace a doctor, clinician, or counsellor.

**What if someone is in immediate danger?**
Call your local emergency services immediately (112 in Europe, 911 in North America, 999 in the UK, or your local equivalent). ChillMate's emergency features are not a substitute for calling emergency services.

**Where can I find more harm-reduction information?**
- [DanceSafe](https://dancesafe.org) (US)
