Privacy Policy

ChillMate is built privacy-first. Your information lives on your iPhone, under your control. We do not run servers that collect your data, we do not use advertising or analytics trackers, and we never sell or share your personal information.

Last updated: 11 June 2026 · Applies to the ChillMate iOS app and this website.

The short version

  • On your device. Your profile, logs, plans, and notes are stored locally on your iPhone.
  • No trackers. No third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.
  • Nothing sold. We never sell or share your personal, health, or sexual-health information.
  • You are in control. You decide what to add, what to sync, and you can delete everything at any time.

What ChillMate can save

  • Your profile, photo, medication notes, trusted contact, home address, settings, and preferences.
  • Private logs, sleep notes, STI test records, plans, journal entries, risk checks, check-ins, and emergency-card details.
  • Optional information you choose to add from Apple Health, Contacts, Photos, or Location Services.

How your information is used

  • To show your private overview, reminders, aftercare prompts, STI follow-ups, emergency shortcuts, and wellbeing reflections.
  • To sync with Apple Health only for the categories you approve in iOS settings.
  • To create encrypted backups only when you turn backup features on.

All of this processing happens on your device. ChillMate does not send your content to the developer.

What ChillMate does not do

  • No ads, no selling personal information, and no sharing of health or sexual-health details for marketing.
  • No medical diagnosis, treatment decisions, dosage advice, or confirmation that a substance, amount, or combination is safe.
  • No messages are sent to your contacts unless you choose to send them.

Where your data is stored

On your iPhone. Data is kept in the app's private storage, protected by iOS file protection and, if you enable it, an extra app lock (Face ID or PIN).

Optional iCloud. If you turn on iCloud backup or sync, ChillMate stores your data in your own private iCloud account through Apple's CloudKit and iCloud Drive. It is protected by Apple's encryption and is accessible only to you. The developer has no access to it.

No developer servers. ChillMate has no backend that receives or stores your personal information.

Device permissions

Each permission is optional and only used for the purpose you grant it:

  • Apple Health — read and write only the categories you allow (such as sleep, heart rate, HRV, workouts, and sexual-activity logging).
  • Contacts — only to let you pick a trusted contact. The lookup happens on your device.
  • Photos — only to set a profile picture you choose.
  • Location — only to attach a location to a log or include your current location in an emergency message you send.
  • Notifications — for the reminders and check-ins you turn on. Discreet wording can be enabled so lock-screen text stays vague.

Payments & tips

ChillMate is free. An optional tip ("Support the developer") is handled entirely by Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple processes the payment; the developer never receives your card or account details. Tips do not unlock features and do not change what data is collected.

Your control & deletion

  • You can delete logs, plans, STI tests, timers, risk checks, journal entries, and your whole account from inside the app.
  • Deleting the app removes its local data from your iPhone. iCloud data can be removed from iCloud settings or from within the app's backup controls.
  • iOS permission controls for Health, Location, Notifications, Contacts, and Photos remain available in the Settings app at any time.

Because ChillMate does not collect your data on a server, there is no remote profile to request, correct, or erase — you already hold and control all of it. If you are in the EU/EEA, your GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection) are satisfied directly through these on-device controls.

Children

ChillMate is intended for adults only. You must be 18 or older to create a profile and use the app.

ChillMate is a wellbeing and reflection tool, not a medical service. It does not diagnose, treat, or tell you whether anything is safe. For STI, PrEP, PEP, medication, mental-health, or substance questions, speak with a qualified professional. If someone may be in immediate danger, call your local emergency number.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date. Material changes will also be noted in the app or its release notes.
