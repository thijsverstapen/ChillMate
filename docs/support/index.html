Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes. ChillMate stores your information on your iPhone. There are no ads, no trackers, and nothing is sold. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I back up or move to a new phone?

Turn on iCloud backup in Settings to save an encrypted backup to your own iCloud Drive, or export an encrypted backup file. On a new device, restore from iCloud or import the backup file during setup.

How do I lock the app?

In Settings → Privacy & lock, turn on Face ID or set a PIN. You can also enable discreet notifications so lock-screen wording stays vague.

How do tips work?

The optional "Support the developer" tip uses Apple In-App Purchase. It is entirely optional, unlocks nothing, and keeps every feature free for everyone.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual entries or your whole account inside the app. Deleting the app removes its local data, and iCloud data can be removed from the backup controls or iCloud settings.

Does ChillMate give medical or dosing advice?

No. ChillMate helps you reflect, plan, and organise. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or tell you whether a substance, amount, or combination is safe. Always confirm anything important with a qualified professional.

Which countries does it support?

ChillMate works anywhere. Help resources are tailored to the country you pick in setup, with Dutch services built in and international fallbacks for everyone else.

In an emergency, do not wait for the app. If you or someone else may be in immediate danger, call your local emergency number now (112 in the Netherlands and most of Europe, 911 in the US, 999 in the UK).
